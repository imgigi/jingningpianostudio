About Me

Jingning Liu

Jingning Liu is a pianist and piano educator currently pursuing her Master's degree in Piano Performance at Miami University (USA). She earned her Bachelor's degree in Music Education from Nanjing Xiaozhuang University, where she received comprehensive training in piano, voice, and erhu. Her academic background has given her a solid foundation in both performance and pedagogy.

With over five years of teaching experience, Jingning has worked with students of all ages—from young children to teenagers and adults—at music studios and educational institutions across China. She offers both private and small-group lessons and emphasizes an individualized teaching approach, adapting her methods to each student's personality, learning pace, and musical goals. Her lessons are designed to be structured yet relaxed, supporting steady and confident progress.

In addition to teaching, Jingning actively participates in music programs, performances, and educational exchanges in both China and the United States. These experiences continue to broaden her musical perspective and inform her teaching practice.

Known for her patience, warmth, and attentiveness, Jingning is especially skilled in working with children and maintaining clear, supportive communication with parents. She believes that piano learning is not only about technical development, but also about cultivating confidence, focus, and a genuine love for music. Through her teaching, she aims to support students as they grow musically and enjoy the learning process step by step.

刘静宁

刘静宁是一位钢琴演奏家和钢琴教育者，目前正在美国迈阿密大学攻读钢琴演奏硕士学位。她在南京晓庄学院获得音乐教育学士学位，在那里接受了钢琴、声乐和二胡的全面训练。她的学术背景为她在表演和教学方面奠定了坚实的基础。

凭借五年多的教学经验，静宁曾在中国各地的音乐工作室和教育机构与各年龄段的学生合作——从幼童到青少年和成人。她提供私人课程和小组课程，强调个性化的教学方法，根据每个学生的性格、学习节奏和音乐目标调整教学方法。她的课程设计既有结构性又轻松，支持学生稳步自信地进步。

除了教学，静宁还积极参与中美两国的音乐项目、演出和教育交流活动。这些经历不断拓宽她的音乐视野，并为她的教学实践提供参考。

静宁以耐心、热情和细心著称，尤其擅长与儿童合作，并与家长保持清晰、支持性的沟通。她相信钢琴学习不仅是技术发展，也是培养自信、专注力和对音乐真正热爱的过程。通过教学，她旨在支持学生在音乐上成长，并一步一步享受学习过程。

Lessons

Course Offerings

课程设置

Trial Lessons

试听课

15-20 min | $25

Trial lessons are available for new students who are interested in learning about the studio's teaching approach. The purpose of the trial lesson is to help both the student and the instructor determine whether the studio is a good fit.

Recommended Ages

Ages 4 and up (with basic communication skills)

Lesson Content Includes

  • An initial assessment of the student's musical background and level
  • A brief introduction to basic piano technique and musical concepts
  • Discussion of learning goals and lesson structure
Following the trial lesson, the instructor will provide recommendations regarding lesson type, duration, and an individualized learning plan. The trial lesson is designed to be relaxed, supportive, and pressure-free.

试听课面向有意了解本工作室教学方式的新学生，旨在帮助学生与老师双方判断是否适合继续学习。

适合年龄

4岁以上（有沟通能力）

试听课内容包括

  • 对学生音乐背景与学习程度的初步评估
  • 基础技巧与音乐的简单体验
  • 学习目标与课程安排的沟通与建议
试听课结束后，老师将根据学生情况提供关于课程类型、时长及学习规划的建议。试听课以轻松、支持性的方式进行，不设任何学习压力。

Private Lessons

私人课程

30 min $35 | 45 min $45 | 60 min $60

Private lessons provide individualized instruction tailored to each student's age, level, and musical goals. Lessons focus on building a strong technical foundation while developing musical understanding and expressive playing.

Recommended Ages

Ages 4 and up (children, teens, and adults)

Lesson Content Includes

  • Fundamental piano technique and healthy hand position
  • Reading skills and rhythm training
  • Repertoire selection and study
  • Musical expression and stylistic understanding
  • Effective practice strategies and goal planning
  • Performance and exam preparation (as appropriate)
Private lessons are ideal for students seeking structured instruction and consistent musical progress.

私人课程根据学生的年龄、水平和音乐目标进行个性化教学，注重在建立扎实技术基础的同时，培养音乐理解力与表现力。

适合年龄

4岁以上（儿童、青少年及成人）

课程内容包括

  • 钢琴基础技巧与手型训练
  • 识谱与节奏训练
  • 曲目选择与学习
  • 音乐表现与风格理解
  • 科学练习方法与学习规划
  • 演出与考试准备（视学生情况而定）
私人课程适合希望获得系统、稳定进步的学生。

Group Lessons

小组课程

45 min | $30/per student

Group lessons provide a collaborative learning environment where students develop musicianship through interaction and shared musical activities. Lessons are designed to help students build musical skills in a relaxed and enjoyable setting.

Recommended Ages

Ages 4 and up (Groups are formed based on age and level)

Lesson Content Includes

  • Rhythm and coordination training
  • Ensemble playing and listening skills
  • Sight-reading practice
  • Fundamental music theory
  • Musical games and creative activities
Group lessons help students build confidence, cooperation, and a genuine interest in music. They may be taken independently or as a supplement to private lessons.

小组课程通过互动与合作的学习方式，帮助学生在轻松愉快的环境中提升音乐素养。

适合年龄

4岁以上（根据年龄和程度分组）

课程内容包括

  • 节奏与协调能力训练
  • 合奏与聆听能力培养
  • 视奏练习
  • 基础乐理知识
  • 音乐游戏与创造性活动
小组课程有助于增强自信心、合作能力和学习兴趣，可单独报名或作为私人课的补充。

Studio Class

Studio 集体课程

Studio classes are held periodically and provide students with performance and enrichment opportunities beyond regular weekly lessons.

Recommended Ages

Ages 4 and up

Class Content May Include

  • Studio recitals
  • Performance classes and in-class performances
  • Peer feedback and group discussion
  • Masterclass-style instruction
Studio classes help students develop stage presence, confidence, and meaningful musical communication skills.

Studio 集体课程定期举行，为学生提供除日常课程之外的演出与拓展学习机会。

适合年龄

4岁以上

课程内容可能包括

  • Studio 音乐会
  • 演奏展示与课堂演出
  • 表演者互评
  • 大师课形式的指导
Studio 课程帮助学生提升舞台表现力、自信心以及音乐交流能力。

Accompanied Lessons

陪练课

Accompanied lessons focus on guided practice and learning supervision to help students reinforce material learned in regular lessons and develop effective practice habits. These sessions emphasize practice support rather than the introduction of new technical or musical concepts.

Recommended Ages

Ages 4 and up (Recommended for students who benefit from structured practice guidance)

Lesson Content Includes

  • Review and reinforcement of lesson material
  • Guidance on practice structure and time management
  • Breaking down practice goals and addressing specific challenges
  • Supervised slow practice, fingering, and rhythm accuracy
  • Development of focus, consistency, and healthy practice habits
Accompanied lessons are offered as a supplement to private lessons and do not replace formal private instruction.

陪练课主要以练习指导与学习监督为核心，帮助学生巩固课堂所学内容，建立良好的练琴习惯。课程重点不在于教授新知识，而在于协助学生高效完成日常练习。

适合年龄

4岁以上（适合需要练习督促与方法引导的学生）

课程内容包括

  • 课堂内容的复习与巩固
  • 练琴流程与时间管理指导
  • 练习重点拆分与问题纠正
  • 节奏、指法及慢练方法的监督
  • 练琴习惯与专注力培养
陪练课可作为私人课程的补充，帮助学生提高练习效率，但不替代正式的私人课程。

Studio Policy

Policies & Guidelines

政策与指南

Enrollment & Scheduling

注册和排课

Lessons are scheduled on a weekly basis at a fixed time.

Enrollment is subject to studio availability, and lesson times are reserved exclusively for each student.

课程采用每周固定时间制，具体时间视工作室空余情况安排。

注册成功后，该上课时间将为学生单独保留。

Attendance & Cancellation

出勤与请假

For lesson cancellations, please provide at least 24 hours' notice.

Missed lessons without proper notice cannot be made up or refunded. Students who attend all scheduled lessons will be eligible for appropriate recognition or rewards. (Each month, up to two absences for illness or personal reasons are allowed without penalty.) Make-up lessons are subject to instructor availability and cannot be guaranteed.

If a lesson is canceled due to instructor reasons, a make-up lesson will be scheduled.

如需请假，请至少提前24小时通知。

未提前请假而缺席的课程，恕不补课或退款，全勤会有适当的奖励。（每月有两次事病假无责取消机会）补课视教师时间安排而定，无法保证一定提供。

如因教师原因导致课程取消，将安排补课。

Class Expectations

课堂要求

Students are expected to arrive on time and come prepared for each lesson.

Respect for the instructor, studio environment, and instruments is required at all times.

Regular and structured practice is an essential part of learning the piano. Students are expected to practice consistently according to guidance provided in lessons to ensure steady progress.

学生应准时到达课堂并做好上课准备。

请尊重教师、工作室环境及乐器设备。持续、有计划的练习是学习钢琴的重要组成部分。

学生需按照课堂要求进行规律练习，以保证学习效果。

Parent Feedback

家长建议栏

Parent feedback is highly valued and helps the studio continually improve.

If you have any suggestions regarding lesson content, practice routines, teaching methods, or other aspects of the studio, please feel free to share them with the instructor.

All feedback will be carefully considered, and adjustments will be made whenever possible to better support the student's learning and growth.

家长的意见对工作室的持续改进非常重要。如果您有任何关于课程内容、练习安排、教学方法或其他方面的建议，欢迎随时向教师提出。

我们会认真倾听并在可能的范围内进行调整，以更好地支持学生的学习和成长。
